# Product Requirements Document: SBrandOps ## 1. Executive Summary SBrandOps is an AI-native Brand Operating System (BOS) designed to unify fragmented marketing tools into a single, cohesive ecosystem. It empowers brands and agencies to Plan, Create, Publish, Engage, Analyze, and Optimize their entire marketing operation. ## 2. Brand Identity & Visual Strategy * **Positioning:** The "Operating System" for modern brand growth. * **Attributes:** Strategic, Analytical, Premium, Modern, Systematic, AI-native, Enterprise-ready. * **Color Palette:** Midnight (#0B1220), Surface (#111827), Primary Blue (#2563EB), Cyan Accent (#06B6D4). * **Typography:** Inter / Manrope (English), Tajawal / Cairo (Arabic). * **UX Principles:** Clear hierarchy, modular cards, data-dense but clean, integrated AI assistance. ## 3. Product Modules 1. **Brand Hub:** Guidelines, assets, personas, and brand voice. 2. **Content Ops:** Campaign planning, AI generation, and approval workflows. 3. **Social Ops:** Multichannel scheduling and engagement tracking. 4. **Ads Ops:** Cross-platform analytics (Meta, Google, TikTok) with ROAS/CPA focus. 5. **SEO Ops:** Keyword planning and content optimization. 6. **Inbox Ops:** Unified communication with AI-powered suggestions. 7. **Automation:** Task management and recurring workflow triggers. 8. **Analytics Hub:** Executive reporting and multi-channel attribution. ## 4. Target Personas * Founders & Marketing Managers * Brand & Content Managers * Social Media & SEO Specialists * Media Buyers & Operations Managers * Agencies managing multiple clients ## 5. Scope of Design ### Phase 1: Marketing Website * Home Page (Hero, Modules, Workflow, Pricing, Footer) * Platform & Module Overviews * Pricing & Book a Demo ### Phase 2: SaaS Web Application (Desktop) * Onboarding & Workspace Creation * Main Dashboard & Module Dashboards (Brand Hub, Content, Ads, etc.) * Specific Workflows: Content Brief Builder, Unified Inbox, Campaign Breakdown. ### Phase 3: Mobile Companion App * Mobile Dashboard & Notifications * Approval Requests & Quick Analytics Snapshots * Unified Inbox (Mobile) ## 6. Technical Requirements * **Multilingual:** Full LTR (English) and RTL (Arabic) support. * **AI Integration:** Visible AI assistant drawer and inline suggestions. * **Enterprise Features:** Permissions, billing, and deep integrations.
